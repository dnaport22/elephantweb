The elephant app team
The elephant app has been developed by Nav Dhuti in partnership with London South Bank University's Sustainability Team and the Division of Computer Science and Informatics within the School of Engineering.
Our mission
It's easy to take small steps to reduce your environmental impact - starting with reducing the consumption of goods! This app will help you find a new home for the things you no longer need, and save them from being thrown away.
Why elephant?
The app was conceived and developed for LSBU's staff and students. The name elephant was chosen because elephants live in harmony with their natural environment, and in doing so help preserve and protect it.
