Welcome to elephant!

This is a sharing app for the London South Bank University staff and student community. Use it to find a new owner for the things you no longer need, and save our planet (and your money) by reusing things rather than buying them new.

elephant allows you to upload or preview available stuff FOR FREE! Please note that the App is moderated for your safety and the items are not immediately visible. To sign up, simply register with your @lsbu.ac.uk email address and log in.

If you want to get something for free

  • Open the app to browse through the available items.
  • When you see something that you like, click on the image, title or description to find out more about the item.
  • If you are interested in the item, write a message to its owner.
  • When the item is no longer available, the owner will delete the item from the app (nothing for you to worry about!).

    • If you want to donate something

  • At the bottom of the screen click on the post item button.
  • You can upload one image of your item, with a title (up to 30 characters) and a description (up to 200 characters).
  • Once you are happy with your item, click on the “Post” button.
  • The item will now be visible to our administration for approval.
    • Should you have an issues whilst using the app, please do get in touch at myelephant.xyz@gmail.com We hope you will enjoy using elephant and thank you for your help in building a more sustainable world!
    Best wishes from the elephant & LSBU teams
